About Me

I was born in Corpus Christi Texas and lived around the South Texas region until I graduated from High School. I signed up for the Air Force before graduating and left a couple months after.

In the Military, I learned logistics from almost every stand point. Holding the chalk for a vehicle to stop to leading the team in missions. From the ground support of mail to huge important military shipments, from food to frozen radar sites in Alaska to food in the molting Middle East, and from the food the President eats to the vehicles he rides in and then to the wasteform the food we loaded the aircraft with offloaded.

Now I am out of the Military and still finding my path. I am taking on this challenge in web developement to better myself and hopefully find that path.

I like Wakeboarding, Snowboarding, Disc Golf, Barbecueing, and Cooking. I am married and have a dog named Suave.
